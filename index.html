I received the Ph.D. degree (supervised by Prof. Ting-Zhu Huang) from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), Chengdu, China, in 2013. From Jan. 2012 to Jan. 2013, I visited Prof. Raymond Honfu Chan as a Joint-Training Ph.D. student at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), Hong Kong, China. I am a Professor with the School of Mathematical Sciences of UESTC. My research interests include remote sensing, image processing, and numerical linear algebra.
